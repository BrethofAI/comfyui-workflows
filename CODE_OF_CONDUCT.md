# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

This list is maintained openly. Contributors and readers are expected to
behave respectfully toward one another and toward the maintainers of the
tools listed here.

## What's expected

- **Honest contributions.** Submit entries with verifiable receipts —
  link the pricing page, link the GitHub repo, link the docs. Don't
  paste marketing copy.
- **Direct critique is welcome, personal attacks aren't.** Calling out
  a tool's licensing problem or anti-developer behavior is fine.
  Calling out individual employees by name isn't.
- **No spam.** Affiliate links, referral codes, and SEO-link-building
  PRs get closed without merge.

## What isn't tolerated

- Harassment of contributors, maintainers, or anyone mentioned in entries.
- Discriminatory language or behavior.
- Posting private communications without consent.
- Sustained disruption of issue threads or PRs.

## Enforcement

The repo maintainer (Brethof AI) reviews reports and may close PRs,
lock issues, or block contributors as needed. For serious incidents,
contact: marketing@brethofai.xyz
